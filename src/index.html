Jonatans Linguine a-la Salsiccia

A simple and delicious pasta course with linguine, salsiccia, and veal stock.

Ingredients:

  • Linguine
  • Salsiccia sausage
  • Veal stock
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Put on pasta water in a medium to large pot over high heat and lightly salt it.
  2. Take large frying pan, drizzle some cooking fat of your choice and put over medium high heat.
  3. Peel and press garlic into the now hot pan.
  4. Place preferred amount of linguine into the now boiling pasta water and boil until al-dente.
  5. Let the garlic saut√© while you chop the salsiccia into semi-thin coins.
  6. Place the chopped sausage into the pan and stir, let it fry until golden brown.
  7. After salsiccia is cooked, in the same pan, add a generous amount of cream and a tablespoon of veal stock and stir.
  8. Add around a tablespoon of pasta-water to the simmering sauce.
  9. Let simmer until slightly reduced, then drain the al-dente linguine and add to the pan.
  10. Lower heat under the pan to medium-low and fold in the linguine until the sauce is thick and creamy.
  11. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  12. Serve hot.
